About My Exam Mentor

Know the platform, the purpose, and the person behind MEM.

About the Product

My Exam Mentor (MEM) is built to simplify exam preparation by organizing authentic past papers in a clean, distraction-free way.

Students can quickly find board-wise, subject-wise, and year-wise papers without wasting time searching across multiple websites.

About the Founder

Hi, I’m Dheeraj Gurrapu, a Grade 7 student studying in an international school.

The idea for My Exam Mentor came from a very personal place. I noticed my sibling struggling to find the right past papers while preparing for exams. The resources were scattered, confusing, or hard to access.

I felt that if this problem existed for my sibling, it probably existed for many other students as well — including my classmates. Since I had the ability to build a solution, I decided to do it.

MEM is created by a student, for students — with the aim of making learning simpler, more organized, and less stressful.

Support

My Exam Mentor is continuously improving. If you find missing papers, incorrect files, or have suggestions, your feedback truly matters.

The goal is simple — support students with the right resources at the right time, and help them prepare with confidence.
