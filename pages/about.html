About My Exam Mentor

Know the platform, the purpose, and the people behind MEM.

About the Product

My Exam Mentor (MEM) is built to simplify exam preparation for students by organizing authentic past papers in a clean, distraction-free way.

Instead of searching multiple sites, students can quickly find board-wise, subject-wise, and year-wise papers — all in one place.

About the Founder

MEM is created by a student who understands the real challenges of exam preparation — confusion, time pressure, and lack of proper resources.

The goal is simple: build tools that are actually useful, easy to use, and designed from a student’s point of view.

Support

My Exam Mentor is continuously improving. If you find missing papers, incorrect files, or have suggestions, your feedback truly matters.

Support is focused on helping students learn better, faster, and smarter — without unnecessary complexity.
